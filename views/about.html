About MET Token

Token Name: MET Token

Symbol: MET

Contract Address:
0xb80b92Be7402E1e2D3189fff261D672D8104b322

Blockchain: Binance Smart Chain (BSC)

MET Token is the in-game currency used for the Paid Slot Machine game. Players can buy MET tokens using BNB at a fixed conversion rate of 1 MET = $1 USD in BNB. You’ll need some BNB in your wallet to cover gas fees.

The MET Token powers all gameplay, payouts, and future upgrades on this platform.
