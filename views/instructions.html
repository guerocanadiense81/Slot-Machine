How to Play

  1. Install MetaMask and connect to Binance Smart Chain (BSC):
    • Network Name: BSC Mainnet
    • RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org
    • Chain ID: 56
    • Symbol: BNB
  2. Add the MET Token to your wallet using the contract address: 0xb80b92Be7402E1e2D3189fff261D672D8104b322
  3. For the paid version, use BNB to purchase MET tokens (1 MET = $1) via our conversion tool.
  4. Spin the reels using your MET credits and win rewards based on matching symbols.
  5. Click “Cash Out” to settle your winnings on-chain (gas fees are covered by your BNB).

Note: You must have sufficient BNB to cover gas fees when buying or cashing out MET tokens.
