🧠 How to Play & Connect Wallet

🔗 Step 1: Connect MetaMask Wallet

  1. Install the MetaMask browser extension from metamask.io.
  2. Create or import a wallet using a secure seed phrase.
  3. Once MetaMask is set up, click the Connect Wallet button in the game.

🛠 Step 2: Add BSC Network to MetaMask

Open MetaMask and click the network dropdown. Select "Add Network" and enter:

  • Network Name: Binance Smart Chain
  • RPC URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
  • Chain ID: 56
  • Currency Symbol: BNB
  • Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com

💰 Step 3: Add MET Token to MetaMask

Still inside MetaMask, click "Import Tokens" and paste the following:

  • Token Contract Address: 0xb80b92Be7402E1e2D3189fff261D672D8104b322
  • Token Symbol: MET
  • Decimals: 18

You will now see your MET balance in your wallet.

🪙 Step 4: Buy MET Tokens Using BNB

Go to the Paid Game and scroll to the "Buy MET" section.

  1. Enter the amount in USD or BNB.
  2. The system will calculate and display:
    • 📈 MET Tokens you will receive (1 MET = 1 USD)
    • 💱 Equivalent BNB required based on real-time rates
    • 💵 USD equivalent
  3. Click Buy Now and approve the transaction in MetaMask.
  4. You’ll receive MET tokens after the BNB is sent to the system wallet.

⚠️ Important Notes

  • You must hold some BNB to cover gas fees on the BSC network.
  • MET tokens are used to play the paid slot machine.
  • Paid game will deduct MET for each spin depending on your bet.

💸 Cashing Out Winnings

  • Click the "Cash Out" button after playing.
  • The system will send winnings (in MET) to your wallet.
  • A small 2% fee may be deducted and sent to the system for sustainability.
  • The backend will process win/loss transactions using smart contracts.

✅ Summary

  • 🎰 Free Game = off-chain credits
  • 💵 Paid Game = real MET tokens & on-chain winnings
  • 💳 1 MET = 1 USD (in BNB, via CoinGecko price feed)
  • 💸 Cash outs & payouts handled on-chain using smart contract
