How to Play

Free Version

Start with $50 credits. Choose your bet by clicking on the bet chips, then click the Spin button. If all three reels match, you win additional credits!

Paid Version (MET Tokens)

  1. Connect your MetaMask wallet.
  2. Buy MET tokens with BNB (minimum 50 MET).
  3. Select your bet and spin.
  4. Winnings are added to your MET balance automatically.
